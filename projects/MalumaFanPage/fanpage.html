Bio

Juan Luis Londoño Arias, professionally known as Maluma, was born in Medellín, Columbia, where he grew up playing several sports and writing song lyrics as a hobby. His composed his first song, "No Quiero", when he was only 15 years old. After recognizing his potential, Maluma started taking singing lessons at 16 with the ambition of debuting as an artist. He adopted the stage name Maluma, combining the first two letters of each member of his family, his mother Marlli, his father Luis and his Manuela sister.

In 2015, he launched his first album, "Pretty Boy, Dirty Boy", which immediately topped the latin album list of Billboard. He eventually released more albums, including "F.A.M.E." and "11:11", in following years featuring popular artists like Shakira, Ricky Martin, and Madonna. In addition to composing music, he also has his own clothing line. According to critics, Maluma has major impacts in latin America and the United States as an urban music artist.
